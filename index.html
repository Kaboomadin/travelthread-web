Everything you need

Built for real travel groups

No public feeds, no algorithm. Just you and your travel crew, sharing moments privately.

📸

Private Photo Feed

Everyone in the group can upload photos. Like, comment, and react with emoji — your own private Instagram for the trip.

💬

Live Group Chat

Text, images, and GIFs in a shared chat. React to messages with emoji. Coordinate dinner, share finds, or just laugh together.

🗺️

Journey Map

Every photo drops a pin. Tap a pin to see the photo and browse the full story of where you went, in order.

✨

Trip Story

When the trip ends, generate a trip story in your choice of style — grid, polaroid, filmstrip, scrapbook, magazine, timeline, and more. Share it publicly if everyone agrees, or keep it private.

🔒

Invite Only

Each trip has a unique invite code. Nobody gets in without it — your memories stay between your group.

📅

Timeline

Scroll through every moment in chronological order. Swipe through the full photo slideshow from the day you started.

Simple by design

Up and running in 60 seconds

No setup, no tutorial. Just create a trip and share the code.

1

Create a trip

Give your trip a name and you're in. A unique invite code is generated instantly — no configuration needed.

2

Invite your crew

Share the code with whoever is joining. They enter it in the app and land straight in your group.

3

Share as you go

Upload photos, chat, react to each other's moments. The map fills up automatically as you shoot on location.

4

Wrap it up

When the trip is over, generate your trip story — a collage of everyone's best shots. Relive it anytime.

Your memories, your rules

Private by default

Everything in TravelThread stays inside your group. There are no public profiles, no discovery feeds, and no surprises.

🔐

End-to-end group privacy

Photos, messages, and your map are visible only to people with your invite code. Nothing leaks out unless your whole group agrees.

🗳️

Unanimous consent to share

Want to share your trip story publicly? Every single member must vote yes. One no vote — or no vote at all — and it stays private.

🗑️

Delete anytime

Remove your own photos at any time. Delete your account and all your data is gone within 30 days — no questions asked.

🛡️

Nobody can share your photos without your consent. The group collage only goes public when every member has voted to share it. If you haven't voted, it stays locked.
